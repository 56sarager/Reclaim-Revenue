How it works

Your Phone Never Goes to Voicemail Again

An AI voice agent answers every inbound call after hours, when you are on a job, or unavailable. It converses naturally with callers, captures contact details, qualifies the work type, and schedules a callback in your calendar without you lifting a finger.

  • Customer calls your regular business number
  • If no one picks up, the call is forwarded to AI after 12-15 seconds
  • It collects the customer's name and contact info and books an appointment
  • The lead is logged in your CRM and you receive a summary notification

How it works

Reviews That Send Themselves

The moment a job is marked complete in your CRM, a personalized review request goes out to the customer automatically via email. No manual follow-up, no awkward asks, and no forgetting. Reviews compound over time and lift your visibility in local search results.

  • The request links directly to your Google Business Profile
